Donna- Virtual Assistant

Donna is an AI powered bot that works with facebook to automate all the functions of a class representatives in institutions of higher learning. Donna Virtual Assistant is a utility app that is equivalent to a personal assistant to a student that informs a student on all the important activities of the University. These activities include being informed about Classes of the day, cancelled classes of the day, makeup classes, CATs, specialized notices from a students faculty and Exams..

The upside of Donna is that the user dont need to download the application to their phone. Once you have a facebook account you simply search Donna and after the simple setup, the user can interactively chat with Donna via messanger app. Finally, Its up all the time and it does not get tired. An upgrade is due for the physically challenged students that will allow use oof voice instructions.

The NEXT big thing from SKIKA

The Proposed smartKIPANDE County Identificaton System

Brief Description Of the Project.

smartKIPANDE is the proposed new security feature addition to the National Idenfication Card proposed to be implemented in phases beginning with the County Government. Once a citizen has registered for an ID meeting the age requirement as outlined in the constitution, he/she will be issued with a smartKIPANDE which is similar to the current National ID but with the addition of a unique QR-Code for identification of that individual.

Once the details of all of applicants have been documented in the Central Government database, Law Enforcement officers are given admin privileges in the smartKIPANDE mobile application to be able to scan a citizens smartKIPANDE QR-Code for verification of the citizen’s Citizenship.

The law enforcement officers can also effectively put out an arrest warrant or an amber alert on a citizen ID which will raise alarms if scanned by any law enforcement officer across the County. The few mentioned features of this system mentioned above are not just theory but practical.

The failsafe for this method of Identification is that even the Citizens will have access to the Application with limited privileges to access their details in case the physical ID is ‘claimed’ Lost.

Problem to be SOLVED

The Manual Security System currently used in the Country has one major flaw which is authentication; a security personnel or a law enforcement officer cannot accurately verify that the holder of an ID card at the time is the rightful owner or if the ID card is original or fake since they most of times just look at the ID card. Also, if a citizen is a wanted fugitive and is on the move from one part of the country to another, the security officers at the other part of the country will hardly notice that the individual is wanted for a felony or crime. It also does away with the excuse of Loss of ID and long queues as during the implementation there will be a provision for the Citizen to Place an Order for the reprint of the card from the comfort of his/her Home.

Reccomendations

The smartKIPANDE security management system has the potential to be a very Safe, Cheap and effective security asset in the State to fighting terrorism. Our recommendation is that it starts small in the County levels from where the Integration of all the County governments will form the National Cyber Security Council. The technology is affordable compared to the Biometric registration that was proposed some time back as we know this will require biometrics scanners that dont come cheap. For this system to operate, it only requires a central Database, hosting servers and a Smart phone with good network connectivity (preferably 4G enabled devices) for faster retrieval of data from the servers. Why not take advantage of the already cheap but powerfull technology.
